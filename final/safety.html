Tornado Safety Tips

Prepare Your Household

  • Create an emergency plan with your family, identifying a safe place to take shelter.
  • Stock an emergency kit with essentials like water, food, flashlight, and first aid supplies.
  • Learn to recognize tornado warning signs, such as a rotating, funnel-shaped cloud.
  • Secure heavy objects that could become projectiles in high winds.

Stay Safe During the Event

  • Seek shelter immediately in a basement, storm shelter, or an interior room on the lowest floor.
  • Avoid windows, doors, and outside walls to minimize risk from flying debris.
  • Protect yourself with sturdy objects like helmets, mattresses, or heavy furniture.
  • If in a vehicle, do not try to outrun the tornado; seek shelter in a sturdy building.

Post-Tornado Actions

  • Check for injuries and provide first aid; seek medical help if needed.
  • Avoid damaged buildings and areas with downed power lines or gas leaks.
  • Listen to local authorities for updates and instructions.
  • Document damage for insurance claims and report it to your provider.
